Platform feature overview

Dashboard

Your central overview page. It brings together the most important academic indicators so you can understand your progress at a glance.

  • Review attendance rate, GPA, recommendation count, and academic grade in one place.
  • View dashboard charts for attendance trends, GPA progress, and course attendance.
  • Use it to spot changes early and move quickly to the tools you need.

Attendance

The attendance area helps you monitor your consistency across the week and across individual courses.

  • Follow the weekly attendance trend to see how regularly you attend.
  • Check the course-by-course attendance bars to find weak subjects early.
  • Use the results to improve discipline and reduce missed sessions.

Recommendation System

This tool evaluates your academic inputs and explains whether a course is a strong match for your current study pattern.

  • Enter the course name, professor name, average study hours, and attendance.
  • Receive a clear result with a reason, match status, and recommendation score.
  • Review your saved recommendation history whenever you want to compare past decisions.

AI Agent

Your interactive assistant for quick questions, study guidance, and support while you work through the platform.

  • Ask questions in natural language and get instant, useful answers.
  • Keep track of conversations through the chat history sidebar and common question shortcuts.
  • Use message actions and file attachments when you need to share or review supporting details.
